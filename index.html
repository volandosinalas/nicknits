Nic Knits

Nic Knits is an indie knitwear brand founded by Nicky Corbett, a self-taught knitter based in Washington, D.C. Nicky creates unique designs using yarn she collects from her travels around the world, including Portugal, the Netherlands and Iceland. To commission a project or inquire about pricing, email nic@nicknits.com. Follow her knitting progress on Instagram at #nicknits.
